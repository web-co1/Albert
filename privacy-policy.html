Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 16, 2025

Information Automatically Collected

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

  • Voluntary Feedback Submissions: If you choose to submit feedback, we collect the text you shared, its full analysis report, and the submission timestamp. This data is used exclusively for service improvement and model training.
  • Anonymized Usage Analytics: We use Google Analytics 4 to understand general site usage patterns, such as which features are most popular. IP anonymization is enabled.
  • Bot Detection Data: We use Google reCAPTCHA v3 to protect the service from automated abuse. This service may collect hardware, software, and user interaction data for security purposes.
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • IP address
  • Pages viewed and time spent on pages
  • Referring website addresses
  • Device identifiers

Information You Provide

We may collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Contact us via email
  • Connect through social media platforms
  • Subscribe to updates or newsletters

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes, including:

  • Providing and maintaining our website
  • Improving user experience
  • Analyzing website traffic and usage patterns
  • Responding to inquiries and communications
  • Detecting and preventing technical issues
  • Complying with legal obligations

Analytics and Tracking

To understand how our project is used and to improve your experience, we use analytics services to help us understand how visitors use our website. We are committed to protecting your privacy, and this section outlines the data we collect and how we use it.

Self-Hosted Analytics

We use a private, self-hosted web analytics application. Our analytics platform is self-hosted, meaning your data is kept on our servers and is not shared with or sold to third-party analytics corporations.

What We Collect

Our analytics tool helps us understand our audience and improve our service by collecting the following information about your visit:

  • Standard Visit Information: We collect standard web analytics data, including the page path you visited, the source that referred you to our site (e.g., Google, Twitter), and your browser's user agent string.
  • Geolocation Data: We determine the country, region, and city of your visit based on your IP address. This information helps us understand our global audience. We also identify network information to detect if traffic is coming from a proxy, VPN, or hosting provider for security purposes.
  • User Type: We distinguish between new and returning visitors using an anonymous identifier stored in your browser's local storage.
  • Web3 Technology: To understand the adoption of decentralized technologies by our user base, we may detect the presence of browser-based Web3 wallets (such as MetaMask or Phantom) and your preferred blockchain type (e.g., EVM, Solana).
  • Bot Detection: We analyze user agent strings and network flags to differentiate between human visitors and automated bots. This ensures our statistics are accurate.

Why We Collect It

The data we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To measure and analyze traffic patterns to determine what content is most useful to our visitors.
  • To improve the user experience by understanding the geographic and technological profile of our audience.
  • To ensure the accuracy of our analytics by filtering out non-human (bot) traffic.
  • To enhance the security and integrity of our service.

Your Privacy and Control

We take your privacy seriously and provide you with control over your data:

  • Anonymous Visitor ID: To differentiate between visits, we use a randomly generated ID that is stored in your browser.
  • For your privacy, this ID automatically expires every 90 days.
  • User Control: You have direct control over this identifier. You can clear your visitor ID at any time by opening your browser's developer console and executing the following command: window.inkwAnalytics.clearVisitorId().

The information we gather is for internal use only and helps us create a better, more relevant experience for our users. Google Analytics

In addition to our self-hosted analytics, we use Google Analytics to track and report website traffic. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous information. You can opt-out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
